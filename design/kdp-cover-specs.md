## Key Guidelines

### Bleed (3.17mm all sides)

- Extend background colors/images to the bleed edge
- Content will be trimmed at this line

### Safe Area

- Keep all critical text and images INSIDE the safe area
- Amazon may trim slightly into the margin

### Barcode

- Amazon places ISBN barcode in bottom-left of back cover
- Keep this area clear (6.35mm margin)
- White or light background required in barcode area

### Spine Text

- Only add spine text if spine is ≥ 14mm wide
- Your spine (23.45mm) CAN have text
- Center text vertically on spine
- Use Spine Safe Area (20.28mm width) for text placement
